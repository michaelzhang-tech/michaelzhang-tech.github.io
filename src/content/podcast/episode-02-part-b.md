## Episode Summary
在数据规模突破百亿级文件、毫秒级延迟成为刚需的今天，金融、EDA芯片设计、4K/8K渲染等场景正面临存储的"不可能三角"挑战——低延迟、海量规模与高效管理难以兼得。

FlexGroup超大卷以“1+1>2”的聚合架构破局，将分散存储资源聚合成整体，在容量、性能、管理间找到最优解。本期节目聚焦NetApp FlexGroup超大卷技术，揭秘如何让集群性能“火力全开”：容量60PB+;文件4000+亿个；性能2-6倍。

## Topics Discussed

Flexgroup best scenarios, Flexgropu ACB(Advanced Capacity Balance) Features in ONTAP 9.16.1 and different use cases.
And as always, our data pulse story times.

## Key Insights

- FlexGroup是否有首选工作负载类型
- ONTAP 9.16.1新增功能之高级容量平衡
- FlexGroup适合哪些具体行业和应用场景
- 持续性，可维护性，易用性，存储各种行业场景如何避坑
- 心跳瞬间分享

## Takeaways

- In ONTAP 9.16.1, Flexgroup has implemented most of the ONTAP features. It is recommended in most cases, like AI training, inference, EDA, etc.
- Flexgroup ACB feature can slice big files into multiple parts and spread into multiple member volumes, as a result, increase the balance of Flexgroup and performance
