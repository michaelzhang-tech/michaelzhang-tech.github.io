## Episode Summary
Is all-flash storage still just about IOPS and latency?

全闪存储的竞争，比拼的只是IOPS和时延吗？

随着数据规模增长、AI工作负载兴起以及网络速度不断提升，企业对存储的要求早已不只是“更快”。
在本期《数据心跳》中，我们深入解析 NetApp AFF A 系列全闪存产品，探讨其背后的架构设计、性能演进，以及 ONTAP 如何在性能、效率与灵活性之间实现平衡。

## Topics Discussed
As AI workloads grow and data infrastructure becomes increasingly demanding, modern storage must deliver more than raw performance.
In this episode of Data Pulse, we explore the NetApp AFF A-Series, its architectural evolution, and how ONTAP balances performance, efficiency, and flexibility in the era of AI and cloud.

## Takeaways

Driven by the wave of AI and the deluge of data, we take a deep dive into NetApp's all-flash product lineup: from the high-end AFF series designed for ultra-fast response scenarios, to the FAS hybrid flash family tailored for hybrid cloud architectures, and the C-series large-capacity arrays pushing the limits of storage, we clearly explain the naming rules and iteration logic of the products. 

On the back of breakthrough hardware upgrades, we further break down the 'hardware-software combo' design thinking behind the hardware revolution, highlighting the dual-drive model of 'user-driven needs + forward-looking technology'.
