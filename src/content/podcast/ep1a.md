## Episode Summary
Why does storage keep getting faster while performance challenges never seem to disappear?

The answer is often architecture, not hardware.

为什么企业级存储越来越快，但用户依然抱怨性能不够？
问题往往不在硬件，而在架构。
在本期《数据心跳》中，我们以 NetApp AFF A 系列为例，聊聊现代全闪存储如何在性能、存储效率、数据服务和扩展能力之间取得平衡，以及这些设计背后的技术逻辑。

## Topics Discussed
In this episode of Data Pulse, we examine the NetApp AFF A-Series and discuss how modern all-flash systems balance performance, efficiency, data services, and scalability.
